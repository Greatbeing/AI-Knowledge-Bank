The Evolution Engine

How knowledge evolves from individual insight to collective wisdom.

1. Fork (Mutation)

Users take existing knowledge nodes and adapt them to specific contexts.

2. Validate (Selection)

Community members test branches in real scenarios. Successful applications increase weight non-linearly.

3. Emerge (Synthesis)

When a branch's weight surpasses the main trunk, the system auto-merges it.
